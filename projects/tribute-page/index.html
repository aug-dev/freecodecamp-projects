Akira Toriyama

Akira Toriyama is one of the main creators responsible for the worlwide popularization of anime and manga.

He is most known for his works Dr. Slump and Dragon Ball, but he is also the creator to a huge number of one-shots published on the Weekly Shonen Jump.

Even though "genius" is a word frequently used to describe Toriyama, it seems that he himself didn't agree with it.

One of the most interesting things to me about him is the fact that he wast not an immediate success. In fact, it took him many iterations to really get things going.

If you happen to be a fan of his works, I strongly encourage you to read more about him on his Wikipedia page.
